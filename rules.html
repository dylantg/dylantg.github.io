FlipFour Rules

FlipFour is a game where players try to get four pieces in a row vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.

Players have a choice of three types of moves: Add, Subtract, or Flip

Add: Drops a piece into the column

Subtract: Removes the bottom piece from the column and shifts all pieces down

Flip: Changes the orientation of the board and pieces shift to the bottom. A player can flip 90 degrees left, 90 degrees right, or flip the board 180 degrees
